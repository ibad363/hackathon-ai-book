## User Scenarios & Testing *(mandatory)*

### User Story 1 - Motivated Reader (Priority: P1)

As a potential reader, I want to read an engaging and informative Preface so that I understand the book's value, what I will learn, and am motivated to continue reading the textbook.

**Why this priority**: The Preface is the reader's first impression and crucial for engagement.

**Independent Test**: A new reader can read the Preface and express a clear understanding of the book's scope, purpose, and target audience, and feel encouraged to proceed.

**Acceptance Scenarios**:

1. **Given** a reader has completed the Preface, **When** asked about the book's main goal, **Then** they can accurately describe it.
2. **Given** a reader is considering the textbook, **When** they read the Preface, **Then** they feel the content is relevant to their interests in Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics.
